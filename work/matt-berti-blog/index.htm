Matt Berti Blog

A weblog about learning Node.js and CSS3.

In production at mattberti.herokuapp.com

This app implements and explains some of the new technologies I was learning at the time. It's designed with HTML5, the SASS preprocessor, and Jade templating engine, and is fully responsive. In the backend I used Node with Express framework, though Travis does all the work with unit testing and deploying to Heroku.
